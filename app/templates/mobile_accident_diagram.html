Desktop / Tablet Recommended

The Accident Reconstruction tool uses an interactive drag-and-drop diagram canvas. It works best on a desktop computer or tablet. You can create a case here on mobile and complete the diagram later on a larger screen.

What This Tool Does

  • Create accident reconstruction cases with date and location
  • Add involved vehicles with directions and notes
  • Record measurements (skid marks, distances, impact points)
  • Upload scene photographs and diagrams
  • Generate a reconstruction packet PDF for the case file

Field Tip

At the scene: photograph vehicle positions, skid marks, and road conditions before vehicles are moved. Note exact measurements. You can enter these into the diagram tool later from a desk terminal.
